# Grade risk with confidence bounds and evidence gates

For one- through four-week horizons, grading uses the 95% upper confidence bounds rather than point estimates: Conservative requires expiration breach at or below 0.5% and path touch at or below 1%; Safe permits up to 2% and 5% respectively; exceeding either Safe limit is Dangerous. Fewer than 100 effective independent paths yields Insufficient Evidence instead of a grade, and five- through eight-week scenario horizons are never graded. Advanced threshold overrides are permitted only when the exported analysis records the overridden values.
